Privacy Policy

A Real Human Being (hereinafter referred to as "ARHB") is a blog website based in the United States. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you use our website, abrdn.co, or our services.

What types of personal information does ARHB collect?

We collect the following types of personal information from you:

  • Contact information, such as your name, email address, and phone number
  • Billing information, such as your credit card number, expiration date, and billing address
  • Website usage data, such as the pages you visit on our website and the links you click
  • Device data, such as your IP address, browser type, and operating system

How does ARHB collect personal information?

We collect personal information from you in the following ways:

  • When you visit our website and provide us with information directly, such as by filling out a form or creating an account
  • When you use our website and we collect information automatically, such as through cookies and web beacons
  • When you interact with us on social media
  • When you purchase our services through Stripe
How does ARHB use personal information?

We use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To provide you with the services you request
  • To send you marketing emails, if you have opted in to receive them
  • To improve our website and services
  • To protect our website and services from fraud and abuse
With whom does ARHB share personal information?

We share your personal information with the following third parties:

  • Stripe, our payment processor
  • Cloudflare, our website hosting provider
  • Third-party service providers who help us operate our website and provide our services

How does ARHB protect personal information?

We protect your personal information by using encryption, limiting access to the information, and regularly reviewing security procedures.

What choices do users have about how their personal information is collected and used?

You have the following choices about how your personal information is collected and used:

  • You can choose not to provide us with any personal information.
  • You can choose not to receive marketing emails from us.
  • You can choose to opt out of cookies and web beacons.
  • You can choose to delete your account and have your information removed from our systems.

To exercise any of these choices, please contact us at admin@arealhumanbeing.co.

Changes to this privacy policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If we make any changes, we will post the updated privacy policy on our website.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at admin@arealhumanbeing.co.

Additional notes
  • Aberdeen is located in the United States and is subject to US privacy laws.
  • Aberdeen does not collect or sell personal information of children under the age of 13.
  • Aberdeen does not sell personal information to third-party advertisers.

Additional disclosures for California residents

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected about them, to request a copy of their personal information, to have their personal information deleted, and to opt out of the sale of their personal information.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at admin@arealhumanbeing.co.

Additional disclosures for European Union residents

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), European Union residents have the right to access their personal information, to have their personal information rectified or erased, to restrict the processing of their personal information, and to object to the processing of their personal information.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at admin@arealhumanbeing.co.

Conclusion

We hope this privacy policy is clear and informative. If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact us.
