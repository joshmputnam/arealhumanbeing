Cookie Policy

A Real Human Being (hereinafter referred to as "ARHB") uses cookies on its website, arealhumanbeing.co. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. Cookies allow websites to remember your preferences and settings, so you don't have to re-enter them every time you visit the website.

Aberdeen uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • To provide you with a more personalized experience on our website
  • To improve the performance and security of our website
  • To analyze how our website is used
  • To deliver targeted advertising to you

You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may prevent you from using all of the features of our website.

Types of cookies used by ARHB

ARHB uses the following types of cookies:

  • Session cookies: Session cookies are temporary cookies that are deleted when you close your browser. Aberdeen uses session cookies to remember your login status and other preferences while you are using our website.
  • Persistent cookies: Persistent cookies are stored on your computer or mobile device until they expire or are deleted. Aberdeen uses persistent cookies to track your return visits to our website and to remember your preferences.
  • First-party cookies: First-party cookies are set by the website you are visiting. Aberdeen uses first-party cookies to provide you with the services and features of our website.
  • Third-party cookies: Third-party cookies are set by a domain other than the website you are visiting. Aberdeen uses third-party cookies to provide you with social media features, analytics, and advertising.

How ARHB uses cookies for advertising

ARHB uses cookies to deliver targeted advertising to you. ARHB may partner with third-party advertising networks to deliver targeted advertising to you. These advertising networks may use cookies to track your browsing activity across different websites and to build a profile of your interests. This profile is then used to deliver targeted advertising to you on other websites.

How to manage your cookie preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences in your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block all cookies, or to allow only certain types of cookies. You can also clear your cookies at any time.

To learn more about how to manage your cookie preferences, please consult the documentation for your browser.

Changes to this cookie policy

ARHB may update this cookie policy from time to time. If we make any changes, we will post the updated cookie policy on our website.

Contact us

If you have any questions about this cookie policy, please contact us at admin@arealhumanbeing.co.
